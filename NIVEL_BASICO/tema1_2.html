Gramática Básica – Presente Simple

Verbos Regulares

I walk
You walk
He / She / It walks
We walk
They walk

Verbos Irregulares

Go → He goes
Have → He has
Do → He does

Uso del Presente Simple

Acciones habituales: I eat breakfast every morning.
Verdades generales: The sun rises in the east.
Rutinas: I go to school every day.

Consejos

Practica regularmente.
Aprende verbos irregulares.
